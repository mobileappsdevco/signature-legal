Support

For help with importing, signing, saving, or exporting a document, contact us by email.

Contact Support

Email parfenovapps@gmail.com with the subject Signature Support.

Include a short description of what you were doing, what you expected, and what happened. You may include the app version, device type, and iOS version. Do not attach confidential documents or signatures unless you have removed sensitive information and intentionally choose to share them.

Before contacting us

  • Confirm that the source PDF or DOCX opens correctly in another app.
  • Review converted DOCX files and scanned pages before adding them.
  • Keep a backup of important source and signed documents.
